Three effects

Pinch Type

Intercepts pinch-to-zoom gestures on mobile and scales text size instead of zooming the page. Text stays in place — just bigger or smaller. No fisheye, no morph.

Scroll Morph

A fisheye reading lens: lines near the viewport center render large and bright, lines at the edges shrink and fade. Creates a natural magnifying effect as you scroll.

Combined

Both effects together. The scroll morph fisheye plus pinch-to-zoom scaling. The full experience.

Accurate Line Breaking

Text is measured and wrapped using @chenglou/pretext for segment-aware, pixel-perfect line breaking on canvas.

$ npm install pinch-type @chenglou/pretext
